Our Beliefs

Mori SDA Youth Choir follows the fundamental beliefs of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, which are rooted in the Holy Bible and centered on Jesus Christ.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the foundation of faith, doctrine, and practice.

The Sabbath

We observe the seventh-day Sabbath (Saturday) as a sacred day of worship, rest, and fellowship in obedience to God’s commandments.

The Second Coming of Christ

We believe in the literal, visible, and soon return of Jesus Christ, which is the blessed hope of the church.
