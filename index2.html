About Mori Youth Choir

Our Beginning

Mori Youth Choir is a Seventh-day Adventist youth choir that came into existence in early 2024 at Mori SDA Church, located in Ranen conference,west-kenya Union Mori Village, North-Kanyamkago Ward, Uriri Constituency, Migori County.

Mission & Purpose

The choir exists to glorify God through music ministry, inspire worship, and spread the gospel message using sacred and meaningful songs rooted in Adventist beliefs.

Activities

The choir takes part in Sabbath services, evangelistic meetings, youth conventions, music festivals, and community outreach programs. Rehearsals emphasize discipline, unity, and spiritual growth.

Vision

Our vision is to nurture talented youth, strengthen faith through music, and positively impact both church and society through Christ-centered harmony.
